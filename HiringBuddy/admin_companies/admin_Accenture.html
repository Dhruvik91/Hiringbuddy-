Accenture

Accenture is a multinational professional services company that provides a wide range of services and solutions in strategy, consulting, digital, technology, and operations. It is headquartered in Dublin, Ireland and has operations in over 200 cities in 120 countries worldwide. The company was founded in 1989 and is one of the largest consulting and professional services firms in the world. Accenture is known for its work in digital transformation, and its services include strategy, consulting, digital, technology and operations services, which are aimed at helping clients to become high-performance businesses and governments. They work with clients across industries, including financial services, healthcare, government, and communications, among others. In 2020, Accenture was ranked as the third largest consulting firm in the world by revenue.
