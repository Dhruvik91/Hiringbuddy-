About Hiring Buddy


Hiring Buddy is an online platform that enables to collages and recruiters to automate end-to-end campus placements. Hiring Buddy helps fresh graduates get their first jobs. enables employers to recruit faster, and helps collages streamline campus placements.


Hiring Buddy recruits Universities onto its platform. Once a University is recruited, all of their students are immediately onboarded following which the placement process begins.
